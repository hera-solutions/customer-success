## Section D — Tenant activity health (cross-classified)

### Method

For each active-billing tenant (Active - Bundle, Active - Premium, Trial), two signals are joined:
- **In-app activity** — any AuditLog mutation in the last 24h. Queried per tenant on the `byTenantIDAndMutationName` GSI of `AuditLog-...-production`. Writes only — pure-read traffic (dashboard views, list browsing) is not in AuditLog.
- **Active associates** — current count of `Staff-...-production` records with `status = "Active"`. Queried per tenant on the `byGroupStatus` GSI (HASH=group, RANGE=status) using `Tenant.group` as the lookup key. This is what we're billing on.

Each tenant lands in one of four quadrants. The naming is deliberately blunt — these are operational categories, not customer-facing language.

| Active staff > 0 | In-app activity 24h | Quadrant | Read as |
|---|---|---|---|
| Yes | Yes | **HEALTHY** | Normal usage. |
| Yes | No | **DARK_BUT_BILLABLE** | Roster is live, owner just hasn't done anything in the app today. Most are normal weekend / weekday-only patterns. Use silence duration to triage. |
| No | Yes | **ZERO STAFF BUT LOGGING IN** | Roster cleared but someone is still in the app. Cleanup mid-transition or considering coming back. Recovery candidate. |
| No | No | **TRULY DARK** | No roster, no in-app. Effectively churned but still on a billing plan. Reconcile billing and have a recovery call. |
